# XXON Rental — Customer Behavior Analytics Dashboard

![XXON Dashboard](xxon-rental-dashboard.png)

## Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analysing customer purchasing behaviour, search patterns, cart abandonment trends, and demographic segmentation for XXON Rental. Built entirely in Microsoft Excel using PivotTables, slicers, COUNTIFS formulas, and dynamic charts, the dashboard enables marketing and sales teams to understand how different customer segments shop, search, and abandon purchases.

---

## Problem Statement
XXON Rental needed clarity on how its 602 customers behave across the purchasing journey — from product discovery through to purchase completion or abandonment. Without structured analysis, marketing decisions were based on assumption rather than evidence. This dashboard transforms raw customer data into actionable commercial insight across eight key business questions.

---

## Tools & Techniques
- **Microsoft Excel** — full dashboard development
- **PivotTables** — dynamic data aggregation across multiple dimensions
- **Slicers** — interactive filtering by Purchase Category, Product Search Method, and Purchase Frequency
- **COUNTIFS formulas** — dynamic KPI card calculations
- **Chart types:** Bar charts, line chart, pie charts, donut chart, area chart
- **Dashboard design** — dark theme UI with consistent colour scheme and KPI header cards

## Business Questions Answered

Gen Z is the dominant purchasing demographic — marketing spend and product positioning should reflect this priority.

## Key Commercial Insights
- **Gen Z drives nearly half of all purchases** — prioritise this demographic in all campaigns
- **Category browsers abandon carts most** — keyword searchers convert at higher rates
- **Female customers are the primary audience** at 58% purchase frequency
- **56 customers purchase multiple times a week** — highest-value loyal segment
- **203 customers purchase only a few times a month** — significant opportunity to increase frequency

---

## Business Recommendations
1. **Target Gen Z first** — 45.68% of purchases; all creative and placement should reflect their preferences
2. **Reduce category browse abandonment** — implement saved cart notifications and retargeting
3. **Invest in keyword search optimisation** — keyword searchers convert better
4. **Build a loyalty programme** for the 56 high-frequency buyers
5. **Increase engagement** for the 203 low-frequency buyers through personalised email campaigns

## What This Demonstrates
This project demonstrates the ability to translate raw customer data into a structured, interactive business intelligence dashboard using Excel — without specialist BI software. The combination of PivotTables, dynamic slicers, formula-driven KPI cards, and multi-chart visualisation delivers commercially actionable findings across the full customer purchasing journey.
